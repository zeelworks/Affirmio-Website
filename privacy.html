2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide core app functionality: Store and manage your affirmations and track your progress
  • Personalize your experience: Maintain your preferences and customization settings
  • Send notifications: Deliver daily reminders based on your chosen schedule

  • Provide customer support: Respond to your inquiries and technical issues

3. Local Data Storage

Local First: All your personal affirmations and content are stored locally on your device using iOS's secure UserDefaults and file system. We do not store your affirmations on remote servers.

3.1 What's Stored Locally

  • Your personal affirmations and any custom content
  • Streak counts, reading history, and achievement data
  • App settings and notification preferences
  • OCR processing results (when using Pro features)

3.2 Data Persistence

We implement multiple backup mechanisms to ensure your data persists across app updates and device restarts, including file-based backups and UserDefaults synchronization.

4. OCR and Photo Processing

For Pro users utilizing OCR (Optical Character Recognition) features:

  • Local Processing: All OCR operations are performed locally on your device using iOS Vision framework
  • No Image Transmission: Photos and documents are never transmitted to our servers
  • Temporary Processing: Images are processed in memory and not permanently stored
  • User Control: You choose which recognized text to save as affirmations
  • Supported Formats: Standard image types (JPEG, PNG, HEIC) up to 5 images per OCR session
